Help What is the Back Pain Monitoring App? Back Pain Monitor is an application that allows you to track your Back Pain & your Medication use. The app will give you insights into what may be causing your pain, and how medication use is affecting it. In addition the app will also notify you if you have any symptoms that could be a precursor for a more severe injury. We are striving to give you an inside look into your pain, understand how to make it better, and make sure you recover safely. When should I see my doctor? Most pain improves gradually with treatment and self-care within a few weeks. You should contact your doctor if:
  • Pain Persists past a few weeks.
  • Spreads down 1 or both legs, especially if pain begins to extened below the knee.
  • Pain causes weakness, numbness, or tingling in 1 or both legs.
  • Pain is acompanied by a fever.
  • If pain is from a fall, blow to the back, or any other type of injury.
